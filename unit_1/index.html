Atelier Creative

Let them be young. Let them be messy. Let them be kids.

Atelier Creative is a space for children and parents all at one address. Children are given the freedom and the resources to explore the creative arts, with or without instruction. Parents can relax or participate, but either way, enjoy the peace of mind that comes with a fulfilled child. Atelier is not a school, nor a daycare, but a community.

See what Atelier has to offer:

  • Supervised classes in arts, crafts, baking and more.
  • Open art studio time.
  • Join parent/child classes.
  • Reserve space for large parties and special occasions.
  • Full cafe and bakery.
